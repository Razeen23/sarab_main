The main types of logistics include inbound logistics (bringing goods into a business), outbound logistics (delivering goods to customers), third-party logistics (outsourcing logistics services), and reverse logistics (handling returns and recycling).

Logistics companies offer a range of services, including transportation, warehousing, inventory management, packaging, freight forwarding, customs brokerage, and distribution.

A customs broker helps businesses navigate the complexities of customs regulations, ensuring that goods are cleared through customs efficiently and in compliance with relevant laws.

Consider factors such as the provider’s experience, reputation, service offerings, technology, network coverage, cost, and ability to meet your specific business needs.
